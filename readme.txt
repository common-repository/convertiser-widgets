Simplifies Convertiser widgets integration into your website.

== Description ==

**NOTE:** The *Convertiser Widgets* plugin requires an account at [Convertiser Network](https://convertiser.com/).

**PHP Version**: This plugin requires at least PHP 5.6+ to be installed

**Features**

1. Automatically replace raw links with affiliated version within your blog posts and comments.
1. Replace product mentions within blog posts and comments with links where mentioned product is currently on sale.
1. Display beautiful product recommendations sets inside your posts.
1. Display price comparison tables inside your blog posts.
1. Display payday offers comparison lists

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install and configure the plugin:

1. Upload the `convertiser-widgets` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the *Convertiser Widgets* plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Configure your settings here: WordPress Admin Area > Settings > Convertiser Widgets
